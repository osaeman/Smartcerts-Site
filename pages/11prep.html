11 Plus Preparation

When it comes to preparing for the 11 Plus entrance exams, practice is key, and starting early is crucial. We recommend beginning preparation at least 12 months before the exam to give students ample time to build their skills. Our tutoring covers all sections of the entrance tests, including English, mathematics, verbal reasoning, and non-verbal reasoning, tailored to both GL and CEM exams.

Our process is structured to ensure thorough preparation:

  • Practice Worksheets: We provide a variety of practice worksheets to help students get familiar with the different types of questions, many of which may be completely new to them. This helps build confidence and understanding of the test format.
  • Mock Papers: Once comfortable with the question styles, students work on mock exam papers to practice completing the test under timed conditions. This helps them improve their speed, accuracy, and ensures they are fully prepared to manage the exam format, including how to fill in their answers correctly.

By focusing on consistent practice and familiarization, we equip students with the skills and confidence they need to succeed in the 11 Plus exams.
