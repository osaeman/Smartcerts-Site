Door Supervisor Course

The Security Industry Authority (SIA) has established standards, and Smart certs Training's Level 2 Door Supervisor course is a recognized qualification that satisfies these requirements. It complies with the pertinent SIA Specifications for Learning and Qualifications, giving candidates the abilities and information needed to submit an application for a SIA license and seek employment as a door supervisor.

In order to finish the 6-day Level 2 Door Supervisor course, potential students must first complete some distance learning prior to the first day of instruction. This initial phase of distance learning functions as a prelude, giving students a foundational understanding and guaranteeing a more efficient and productive training experience during the in-person sessions.

A current and valid First Aid or Emergency First Aid certificate that satisfies the requirements of the Health and Safety (First Aid) Regulations 1981, as specified by the SIA, is also a must for anyone enrolled in Door Supervision or Security Officer training. After the security course begins, this certification must be valid for at least a full year.

In order to guarantee a more secure and organized work environment, the SIA highlights how crucial it is for learners to possess the necessary First Aid or Emergency First Aid certifications.
