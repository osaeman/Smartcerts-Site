First Aid Courses

First Aid Courses in Luton

A wide selection of First Aid courses that are OFQUAL Regulated and approved by the Awarding Organization are provided by Smart certs Training in Luton. Our courses provide participants with three-year First Aid certificates, which guarantee that they are prepared to handle emergencies and deliver critical care in a variety of settings.

Emergency First Aid At Work (EFAW)

The Health and Safety (First Aid) Regulations of 1981 are met by the one-day Emergency First Aid at Work course. Perfect for people looking for career opportunities in the construction and security industries, as well as those working in low-risk environments like offices or retail. The purpose of this Level 3 training is to advance and formally acknowledge participants' first aid knowledge, comprehension, and abilities in the workplace.

First Aid At Work (FAW)

The Level 3 First Aid at Work course is taught via a combination of self-study and two training days, or blended learning. This course is more advanced than the EFAW basic training. According to the Health and Safety at Work Act of 1974, this extensive course guarantees that successful participants become qualified first aiders in the workplace. Companies are required to determine if their workplace requires first responders and to see to it that they are properly trained.

Using a blended learning approach, a portion of the course is revised via online learning while the remaining material is covered in two days of in-person instruction. Key lifesaving techniques, non-threatening situation handling techniques, and the ability to identify and treat a variety of diseases and injuries will all be taught to participants. An assessment consisting of multiple-choice questions wraps up the course.

Paediatric First Aid (PFA)

In particular, the Level 3 Paediatric First Aid course covers both theoretical and practical training in First Aid techniques for infants 0 to 1 and children 1 to puberty. Through the training, participants will be equipped to assess and manage emergency situations involving young children and infants as skilled paediatric first aiders.

Professionals that work with or provide care for young children and infants, such as teachers, playgroup leaders, childcare providers, au pairs, nannies, childminders, and those pursuing an NVQ in childcare and education, should take this course.
