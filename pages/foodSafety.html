Food Safety Course

Food Safety Course in Luton

Smart certs Training's Level 2 Food Safety Course in Luton is a hybrid of online and classroom instruction intended to advance the catering industry's best food safety and hygiene standards. People who work in pubs, hotels, restaurants, hospitals, care facilities, and schools—anywhere food is prepared, handled, and served—should take this course.

LEVEL 2 FOOD SAFETY – 1 DAY TRAINING COURSE:

The Level 2 Food Hygiene and Safety Course aims to provide delegates with a basic awareness of good hygiene and safety practices in the food industry, specifically focusing on employees involved in preparing, cooking, and handling food in catering settings.

This training is relevant for food handlers employed in catering or hospitality, including hotels, restaurants, cafes, bars, fast food outlets, takeaways, kitchens, hospitals, schools, and colleges.

The main aim of the course is to provide candidates with fundamental understanding of food safety procedures in the catering sector, including possible risks, hazards, and economical control methods. Taking the Level 2 Award in Food Safety in Catering can be done as a stand-alone certification or as a component of a larger training program.

Who Is The Course For?

The Level 2 Award in Food Safety in Catering is aimed at caterers and other food handlers, including those already working in the catering industry and those preparing to work in it.

What Does The Course Cover?

The qualification makes it clear that everyone who stores, prepares, cooks, serves, or handles food is responsible for keeping it safe. The Food Standards Agency thinks that the course's topics are very important for keeping up good practices in making safe food. Some important topics are:

Introduction To Food Safety

  • Food Poisoning and its Control
  • Food Premises and Equipment
  • Cleaning and Disinfection
  • Microbiological Hazards
  • Food Safety Enforcement
  • Food Handlers and Personal Hygiene
  • HACCP from Purchase to Service
  • Contamination Hazards and Controls

Course Benefits

If students successfully complete the Level 2 Award in Food Safety in Catering, they will be able to find ways to make catering services safer and put those changes into action. By learning these things, people can play a big part in keeping food safety standards high and protecting consumers' health and well-being.
