Key Stage 1 & 2

Building a strong foundation in math and English from an early age is crucial for a childâ€™s overall success, both in school and beyond. Our tuition program for KG 1 and 2 focuses on introducing young learners to the essential basics in a fun and engaging way. At this stage, we emphasize helping children develop a deep understanding of core concepts, setting the stage for future academic growth

  • Learning Numbers and Simple Math: Through interactive activities, children will begin to understand numbers, counting, and basic addition and subtraction, making math fun and approachable.
  • Developing Mental Math Skills: Simple exercises will help your child begin to think and solve problems quickly, building their confidence in handling numbers.
  • Building Strong Spelling Skills: Children will be introduced to basic phonics and simple word spelling, fostering early literacy.
  • Improving Handwriting: We guide children through the proper formation of letters, helping them practice neat and clear handwriting.
  • Enhancing Reading Comprehension: With stories and simple texts, we encourage children to understand and enjoy reading, improving their ability to grasp meaning from what they read.

By focusing on these foundational areas, we ensure that your child gains the confidence and skills they need to thrive in their academic journey.
