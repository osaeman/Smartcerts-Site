Key Stage 3 & GCSE

AQA, OCR and Pearson Edexcel

For students in KG 3 and GCSE, we offer comprehensive tutoring in English (both language and literature), mathematics, and science, covering both Combined Science and Triple Science. Our program is designed to align with the key exam boards, including AQA, OCR, and Pearson Edexcel, ensuring that all the required material is thoroughly covered.

Beyond teaching subject content, we focus on developing essential study skills tailored to each student’s needs:

  • Note Organisation: We guide students on how to structure their notes in a clear and effective way, making it easier to review and retain information.
  • Revision Planning: We assist in creating personalized revision schedules that maximize study time, helping students to stay on track and prepare efficiently for exams.
  • Revision Techniques: Based on individual learning styles, we recommend the best revision methods, whether it’s mind maps, flashcards, or practice tests, to ensure effective learning and memory retention.
  • Guidance on Subject Choices: For students approaching GCSE and A-levels, we offer advice on selecting the right subjects based on their strengths, interests, and future goals.
  • Support with Work Experience Applications: We also provide assistance with work experience opportunities, helping students gain valuable insights into future career paths.

For any type of training and services. Browse our website and fill out the contact form to schedule a free, no-obligation meeting.
