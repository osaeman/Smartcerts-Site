Security Guarding Course

4 Days Training Course

Security Guarding Course, Seucrity Guard Course

The Security Industry Authority's (SIA) requirements are met by the accredited Level 2 Security Guarding course provided by Smart certs Training. It is in line with the pertinent SIA Specifications for Learning and Qualifications, giving people the abilities and information they need to apply for a SIA licence and find employment as security guards in the UK's private security sector.

This course is perfect for people looking for work in a variety of static locations, such as banks, retail establishments, schools, hospitals, residential areas, construction sites, and shopping malls. At these fixed locations, people can carry out security tasks if they have a Security Guarding license.

It's crucial to remember, though, that owners of Security Guard licenses are not allowed to work in establishments that sell alcohol or in controlled entertainment venues. People must take a Door Supervisor course in order to receive additional training for such roles.

Candidates must also possess a current, valid First Aid or Emergency First Aid certificate that complies with the Health and Safety (First Aid) Regulations 1981 in order to be eligible for Door Supervision or Security Officer training. Every learner must possess the necessary First Aid or Emergency First Aid certifications, according to the SIA. After the security course begins, this certificate needs to be valid for at least a year.

Individuals can start a fulfilling career in the private security sector and guarantee safety and protection in a variety of settings throughout the UK by fulfilling the requirements and earning the Level 2 Security Guarding qualification along with a current First Aid certificate.
