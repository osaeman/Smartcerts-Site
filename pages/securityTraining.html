Security Trainer Course

A certified course, Smart certs Training's Level 3 Delivering Conflict Management Security Training Course is made to satisfy the needs of instructors who want to offer scenario-based conflict management instruction. It is especially helpful for trainers in customer-facing positions and can be implemented in a wide range of industries. It also satisfies the prerequisites set forth by the Security Industry Authority (SIA) for trainers offering license to practise qualifications, which include providing scenario-based conflict management training. Trainers for door supervisors and other front-line employees fall under this category.

LEVEL 3 DELIVERING PHYSICAL INTERVENTION TRAINING COURSE:

The Level 3 Delivering Physical Intervention course is also an accredited qualification, specifically developed for individuals who wish to teach physical intervention skills to personnel in the private security industry. This certification is necessary for trainers who want to instruct the well-liked SIA Door Supervision course in order to teach the Physical Intervention (PI) unit of the Door Supervisor course.

This course is perfect for anybody who want to teach people in the private security industry physical intervention techniques. The PI unit of the Door Supervisor course will also require this qualification for trainers hoping to teach the SIA Door Supervision course.

The two Level 3 courses are essential in giving trainers the skills and knowledge they need to teach people in the private security sector conflict management techniques and physical intervention techniques. With these credentials, trainers can make a big difference in improving security and safety in many different industries and give staff members the necessary abilities to deal with difficult circumstances sensibly and successfully.
